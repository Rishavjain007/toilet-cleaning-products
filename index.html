FAQ

Questions about toilet cleaning

Is ShineHome safe for septic tanks? +
Yes, when used as recommended, ShineHome toilet cleaner is safe for most standard septic systems. Do not overuse or mix with other chemicals in the same bowl.
Can I use it on coloured or designer toilets? +
It is safe on most ceramic surfaces. For coloured or decorative finishes, test on a small area first and avoid leaving for more than 10 minutes.
How is it different from ordinary phenyl? +
Phenyl is mainly for floors and general disinfection. ShineHome toilet gel is formulated specifically for toilet bowls, with strong descaling and germ-kill action that clings to vertical surfaces.
